Umbreon

Umbreon (Blacky) is a Dark-type Pokémon introduced in Generation II. It evolves from Eevee when leveled up with high friendship during the night (in the main series games), or when leveled up with high friendship with a Moon Shard in the Bag (in Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness). It is one of Eevee's final forms, the others being Vaporeon, Jolteon, Flareon, Espeon, Leafeon, Glaceon, and Sylveon. It is a starter Pokémon in Pokémon Colosseum alongside the other Generation II Eeveelution, Espeon.
